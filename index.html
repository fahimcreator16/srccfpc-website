About Club

Club History

Photography and videography are arts that breathe life into frozen memories. With the vision of preserving these moments, a group of talented young individuals founded this club on October 27, 2018— 'SRCC Film & Photography Club'. Over time, many new faces have joined this family. With their creativity and dedication, the club has flourished and earned great distinction. Through the language of our lenses, we don't just capture images; we tell the stories of human struggle, culture, sports, joy, and sorrow. SRCC Film & Photography Club is not just a club; it is the heartbeat of a group of dreamers.

Achievements

Our presence in the sky of success is constant. Every year, we serve as the ‘Media Partner’ for over 250+ festivals, sports events, orientations, and ceremonies. Captivated by the magic of our lenses, organizers have repeatedly honored us with various awards. Furthermore, our skilled club members have showcased their talent in numerous national and international competitions, bringing home prestigious accolades. We carefully preserve every golden memory of our college and deliver those emotions to the people through our work. Each award we earn is more than just a trophy; it is a testament to our relentless hard work and passion.
